# AXon Brand

AXon is the platform name. **Transport Operations Hub** is the workspace name presented beneath or alongside it where context is useful.

## Assets

- `public/brand/axon-mark.svg` is the primary gradient mark for light and dark product surfaces.
- `public/brand/axon-mark-light.svg` is the white reverse mark for dark imagery where the primary mark lacks contrast.
- `public/brand/axon-app-icon.svg` is the rounded dark-background app and Apple touch icon.
- `public/brand/favicon.svg` is the compact browser icon.

Use the mark at a legible size for its context and retain clear space around it. Keep transparent SVG backgrounds intact; do not place the full brand-board image, tagline composition, or product-icon examples in the application UI. Do not distort, rotate, recolour, or add effects that reduce the mark's legibility.

## Product Usage

The sidebar uses the compact app icon with visible AXon and workspace naming when expanded. The login screen uses the primary mark, AXon, and the Operations Intelligence Platform descriptor. Browser titles use `Page | AXon`, with `AXon — Transport Operations Hub` as the default.

The existing semantic blue tokens remain the source of product accent color. Do not replace destructive, warning, success, muted, or focus semantics with brand blue. Avoid decorative animation and preserve current focus treatment and contrast in both themes.

## Future Direction

The brand-board product icons are reference material for a future coherent design-system enhancement. Current feature navigation remains on Lucide icons to avoid mixing icon families prematurely.
